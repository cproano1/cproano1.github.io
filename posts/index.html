Coding Poker

Welcome to my blog! Who needs cards when you can conveniently play casino games on your computer? Well for that reason, I attempted to code a simplified version of Poker on Python. Initially, I imported random and created the lists suits (containing the four suits), faces, hands, and highest_card. Faces has the integer values from 1 to 14, representing the card values, so that the face cards are highest (Ace is 14).
